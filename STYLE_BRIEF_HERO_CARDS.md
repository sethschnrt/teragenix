# Teragenix Hero Card Style Brief

Use this as the visual target for original assets. Do not use or reference competitor images directly.

## Card Structure
- 4 cards inside a shared white tray/container
- Each card has:
  - soft pastel top panel
  - subject image overlapping the top edge slightly
  - white gap separator
  - light grey content bar at bottom
  - small arrow on right
- Rounded corners throughout
- Clean spacing, minimal shadows, premium feel

## Image Direction
- Premium AI brand/editorial imagery
- Attractive adults, clearly 21+
- Mixed ages, varied faces, no same-face drift
- Realistic skin texture, light asymmetry, believable eyes
- Soft studio lighting, luxury wellness feel
- No stock-photo vibe, no corporate headshot vibe, no fitness-ad cheese
- Minimal product presence for now, less vials the better
- No pills, capsules, syringes, or medical devices in hero cards

## Backgrounds
- Pastel mint
- Pastel ice blue
- Warm cream/peach
- Soft sage

## Copy Direction
- Benefit-led, not lab/B2B
- Themes like fat loss, recovery, longevity, vitality
- Avoid research-supply language in hero and trust bar
